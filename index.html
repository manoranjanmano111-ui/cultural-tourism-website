🎭 Festivals you can join 🎉

Danda Nacha

The ancient ritualistic dance of penance, vibrant colors, piercing body movements & mythological storytelling. Celebrated across Ganjam in April-May.

Join with locals →

Raja Parba

Three-day festival celebrating womanhood & monsoon. Experience traditional swings, pithas, folk songs, and the joyous rural spirit of Odisha.

Mid-June special →

Thakurani Yatra

Largest biennial festival of Ganjam, grand procession of Goddess Budhi Thakurani. Street theatre, music, and mass gathering like no other.

March/April next edition →
